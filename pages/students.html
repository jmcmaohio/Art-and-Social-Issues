Welcome to the Art & Social Issues in American Culture Website.  As you explore the images and materials on this site we invite you to reflect on how they relate to your experience.  

You might ask yourself: Are there social issues I can identify in my own community? Are the same issues as prevalent today as they were fifty or one hundred years ago? What positive strategies might I use to effect social change? What art today communicates social messages?  How did artists featured on this Website identify and exhibit issues of concern, and how did the world react?  Did their work make a difference?

We welcome your reactions and suggestions and invite you to let us know what you think by clicking on Contact Us on the navigation bar and use the form on that page to help us in making this Website challenging and useful.
